## üÜò STUCK? COMMON ISSUES:

### Can't find logo to click:
- Look in top-left of header
- OR: Click Navigator panel ‚Üí Find "Logo" or "Brand"

### Find/Replace not working:
- Make sure you're in Designer (not CMS or Pages view)
- Try Cmd+F (Mac) or Ctrl+F (Windows)
- Search box should appear at top

### Can't change button links:
- Click button
- Right sidebar ‚Üí Look for "Link Settings"
- OR: Right sidebar ‚Üí Settings tab ‚Üí Link field

### Can't publish:
- Make sure you saved all changes
- Check you're on the right workspace
- Try clicking Publish again

### Domain not showing:
- Go to Project Settings ‚Üí Hosting
- Check domain is added
- May need to wait for DNS (5-60 min)
