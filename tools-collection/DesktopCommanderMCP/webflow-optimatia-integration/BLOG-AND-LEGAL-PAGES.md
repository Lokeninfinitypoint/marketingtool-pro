### For Legal Pages:
1. **Use clear, simple language** (not legalese)
2. **Break into short sections** with headings
3. **Use bullet points** where possible
4. **Include examples** to clarify
5. **Add table of contents** for long pages
6. **Keep updated date** visible at top
