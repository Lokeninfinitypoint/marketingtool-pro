What is an SEO audit?

An SEO audit is the process of reviewing your online assets to make sure your site is performing the best it possibly can in search results. This includes finding out:

  • Strengths and successes to harness or scale.
  • Mistakes to fix and low-hanging fruit to snatch up.
  • What’s not working that you should do less of.
  • What’s not working that needs to be improved.

The thing is, there are so many facets to SEO that this process can become overwhelming, fast. We’ve simplified the SEO audit into 10 core steps in this post, but if you have a large website, you might also consider separating out your SEO audits into categories. Read on for some SEO audit types.

Off-page SEO

Technical SEO audit
