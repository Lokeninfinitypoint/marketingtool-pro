# Account management

Account management

Managing your Waduit account is straightforward. Use this page to learn how to update your profile, manage team members, adjust notification preferences, and maintain security.

### Update your profile

You can update your personal details, email address, and password from the Profile settings in your Waduit dashboard.

### Manage team members

Invite colleagues to collaborate on your campaigns, assign roles, and manage their permissions to control access.

### Security settings

Enable two-factor authentication and review account activity regularly to keep your account secure.
