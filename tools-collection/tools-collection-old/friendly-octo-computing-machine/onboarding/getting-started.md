# Getting started

## Welcome to Waduit

Waduit is your PPC copilot that helps you manage and optimize pay‑per‑click campaigns with ease. This guide will get you set up and comfortable using the platform.

### Creating your account

1. Go to **waduit.sbs** and click **Sign up**.
2. Enter your business email and create a secure password.
3. Verify your email address and log in to the dashboard.

### Navigating the dashboard

After logging in you’ll see your main dashboard. Here you can:

* View high‑level performance metrics for all your campaigns.
* Access tools like Audit, Performance reports, Budget planning, Ad testing, Bidding strategies and Quality score analysis.
* Jump to manage specific assets like Ads, Campaigns, Keywords and more via the left‑hand navigation.

### Next steps

Once you’re familiar with the dashboard, head to our **Quick start** page to launch your first campaign.
