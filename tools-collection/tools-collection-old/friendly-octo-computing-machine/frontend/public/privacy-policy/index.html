Privacy at Waduit

Privacy Policy

We built Waduit to give PPC teams clarity—without compromising the security and privacy of your data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

  • Effective date: 5 April 2025
  • Last reviewed: 5 April 2025
  • Entity: Waduit (Infinity-), F-12 Govindam Tower, 1st Floor, Jaipur 302012, Rajasthan, India

Information we collect

Waduit collects the minimum information needed to operate collaborative PPC workspaces and provide support. We do not sell personal data.

Account and workspace details

  • Contact information such as name, business email, company, and role.
  • Workspace configuration, user permissions, and settings you choose.
  • Authentication metadata (sign-in timestamps, IP addresses, and audit logs) used for security and troubleshooting.

Performance and usage analytics

  • Event data on how features are used inside Waduit to improve the product and prioritize roadmap decisions.
  • Device and browser characteristics captured through first-party cookies to maintain secure sessions.

Connected advertising accounts

When you connect Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, or other PPC platforms, Waduit accesses campaign metadata and performance metrics strictly to power alerts, Optimization Queue recommendations, and reporting. We never modify your advertising accounts without explicit authorization.

How we use your information

  • Deliver, maintain, and improve Waduit features, including collaborative briefs, monitoring, and automation.
  • Provide onboarding, training, and technical support when you contact us.
  • Secure the platform by detecting suspicious activity, enforcing access controls, and auditing changes.
  • Send important service updates, billing notices, and product announcements. You can opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Comply with legal obligations, tax requirements, and enforce our agreements.
We rely on your consent (for marketing emails and optional cookies), contract performance (to deliver the service), and legitimate interests (to keep the platform secure and reliable) as legal bases for processing.

When we share data

We only share information with trusted processors that help us deliver Waduit. Each processor is bound by confidentiality and security obligations.

  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, databases, monitoring) located in the European Union and United States.
  • Communication tools for email delivery, help desk operations, and in-product chat.
  • Payment and billing partners when you convert to a paid plan.

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Waduit, our customers, or others.

Retention and deletion

Workspace data is retained for the duration of your subscription. We delete or anonymize customer data within 60 days of account closure unless longer retention is required by law. Support tickets and audit logs may be stored for up to 24 months to maintain historical accuracy and compliance.

If you need an earlier deletion, contact privacy@waduit.sbs and we will coordinate a secure erasure.

Security practices

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest for all customer data.
  • Role-based access controls with audit logging for every change.
  • Regular vulnerability scanning, penetration testing, and security reviews.
  • Least-privilege access policies for employees and contractors.

We encourage customers to enable multi-factor authentication and follow least-privilege principles inside Waduit. If you discover a vulnerability, email security@waduit.sbs.

Your rights and choices

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information from within your account settings.
  • Export a copy of workspace data upon request.
  • Request deletion of personal data, subject to legal or contractual limitations.
  • Opt out of marketing communications using the unsubscribe link in each email.

Depending on your location, you may also have additional rights under GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, or other privacy laws. Submit requests to privacy@waduit.sbs, and we will respond within 30 days.

Policy updates

We review this policy whenever we introduce new features or change our data practices. We will notify administrators by email and update this page with the revised effective date.

Continued use of Waduit after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
