One backlog for every PPC improvement

Optimization Queue turns scattered to-do lists into a repeatable workflow. Analysts submit ideas, specialists pick up the work, and stakeholders approve changes—all without leaving Waduit.

Prioritize what moves the needle Score every optimization by impact and effort so teams focus on the changes that drive revenue.
Automated ownership Assign tasks to the right channel expert and notify them in Slack or email instantly.
Proof of performance Attach before-and-after metrics to show stakeholders why an optimization succeeded.

Typical optimization flow

  1. Alerts or analysts flag an opportunity directly inside Optimization Queue.
  2. The task is prioritized, assigned, and given a due date.
  3. Specialists implement changes and log annotations from the same interface.
  4. Stakeholders review results, add feedback, and archive the item for future reference.
