# Reporting

### Reporting overview

The Reporting tool lets you create detailed reports about your campaigns, keywords and ads. Use it to track performance over time, share results with stakeholders, and make data ‑ driven decisions.

#### What it does

* Generate customizable reports with metrics like clicks, impressions, conversions and spend.
* Schedule recurring reports to be delivered automatically to your team.
* Export data to CSV or PDF for further analysis.

#### How to use it

1. Open the **Reporting** tool in the Tools section.
2. Choose the accounts, campaigns or ad groups you want to include.
3. Select the metrics and date range for your report.
4. Save your report and schedule it for regular delivery if needed.

By keeping stakeholders informed with clear, concise reports, you can improve transparency and ensure everyone stays aligned on performance goals.
