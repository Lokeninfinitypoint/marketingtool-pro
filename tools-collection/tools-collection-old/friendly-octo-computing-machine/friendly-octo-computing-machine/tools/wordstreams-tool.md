# WordStream's tool

### What it does

The WordStream’s tool integrates with Waudit.sbs to provide robust auditing and optimization for your Google Ads campaigns and website performance. It analyzes keywords, ad copy, and budget allocations to deliver actionable recommendations and identify areas where your campaigns can be improved. Use WordStream’s insights to sharpen your advertising strategy and drive better ROI.

### How to use it

* Connect your WordStream account or sign up if you don’t have one.
* Go to the WordStream’s tool under Tools in your Waudit.sbs dashboard.
* Select the account or campaign you wish to audit.
* Review WordStream’s recommendations on keywords, ad copy, budget distribution, and landing pages.
* Apply the suggested optimizations and monitor performance using other tools (Audit, Performance, Budget, Ad testing, Marketb, Vaizle, SEISO) for a comprehensive view of your marketing efforts.
