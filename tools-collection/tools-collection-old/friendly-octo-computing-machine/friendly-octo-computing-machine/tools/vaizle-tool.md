# Vaizle tool

### What it does

The Vaizle tool integrates with Waudit.sbs to provide competitive analytics and audits for your digital marketing efforts. It evaluates your website and Google Ads performance against industry benchmarks and your competitors, helping you identify areas of improvement. Use Vaizle to understand how your marketing stack stacks up in your niche, which channels drive the most engagement, and where your campaigns might be wasting spend.

### How to use it

* Connect your Vaizle account or sign up for an account if you don’t have one.
* Navigate to the Vaizle tool under the Tools section of your Waudit.sbs dashboard.
* Choose the campaign or channel you want to analyze (e.g., social media, website performance, Google Ads).
* Review the analytics dashboards and recommendations generated by Vaizle, focusing on competitor comparisons and industry benchmarks.
* Combine Vaizle insights with data from the Audit, Performance, Budget, Ad testing, and Marketb tools to get a comprehensive view of your marketing effectiveness.
