# SEISO tool

### What it does

The SEISO tool integrates with Waudit.sbs to provide in-depth auditing and optimization of your Google Ads accounts. It analyzes underperforming keywords, ad copy, wasted spend, and performance metrics to deliver a comprehensive report with recommendations for improvement. SEISO helps you identify opportunities to reduce costs and improve ROI across your campaigns.

### How to use it

* Connect your SEISO account or sign up for one if needed.
* Navigate to the SEISO tool from the Tools section on your Waudit.sbs dashboard.
* Select the Google Ads account you want to audit.
* Review the SEISO dashboard for insights on wasted spend, underperforming keywords, search terms, and budget allocation.
* Use the recommendations to optimize your campaigns, and combine insights with other tools (Audit, Performance, Budget, Ad testing, Marketb, WordStream’s, Vaizle) for complete visibility.
