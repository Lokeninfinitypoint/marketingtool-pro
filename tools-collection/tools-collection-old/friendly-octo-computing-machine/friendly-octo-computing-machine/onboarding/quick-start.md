# Quick start

## Quick start

To get up and running quickly with Waduit, follow these steps:

1. **Create your first campaign** – Go to the Campaigns tab and click “New Campaign”. Choose your objective and set your budget and bid strategy.
2. **Add keywords** – In the Keywords section, add relevant search terms that match your products or services.
3. **Write ads** – Use the Ads manager to craft compelling ad copy and upload creatives. Make sure your headlines and descriptions are clear and enticing.
4. **Monitor performance** – Use the Performance and Reporting tools to track impressions, clicks, conversions and cost. Adjust your campaigns based on the data.

For more details, explore the Tools and Manage sections of the help center.
