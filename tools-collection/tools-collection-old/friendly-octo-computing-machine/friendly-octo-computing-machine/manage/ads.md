# Ads

### Ads overview

Waduitâ€™s Ads management tool allows you to view and edit all the ads in your account. You can pause, resume, or delete ads, and see performance metrics like CTR, conversions, and cost.

### Managing your ads

* **Review performance:** Use the metrics table to monitor clicks, impressions, conversions, and spend.
* **Edit ad content:** Open an ad to update the headline, description, URLs, or ad extensions.
* **Pause or resume:** Quickly pause underperforming ads or resume successful ones.
* **Duplicate ads:** Create variations by duplicating an existing ad and tweaking the copy or targeting.

### How to edit ads

1. Navigate to the _Ads_ section under **Manage**.
2. Use the filters to find ads by campaign, ad group, or status.
3. Click on an ad to open the edit panel.
4. Update the text, URLs, or other settings.
5. Save your changes and monitor performance in the metrics table.

### Tips

* Use consistent messaging across your ads to reinforce your brand.
* A/B test different headlines and descriptions to see what resonates.
* Keep an eye on disapproved ads and fix any policy violations promptly.
