Product overview

Everything you need to orchestrate PPC

Waduit unifies campaign planning, execution, and optimization so performance marketers can focus on creative strategy, not spreadsheets.

Core workspaces

Campaign Studio

Launch and manage Google Ads and Microsoft Advertising campaigns from a guided workspace that enforces naming, budgets, and approvals.

Insights Desk

Track pacing, budget health, and conversion trends with dashboards stakeholders can interrogate without building custom reports.

Optimization Queue

Turn optimization ideas into an accountable backlog so every test, bid update, and cleanup has an owner and measured outcome.

Capabilities marketers rely on

  • Workspace-level permissions for agencies and in-house teams.
  • Automated data hygiene checks that flag anomalies before they impact spend.
  • Reusable brief templates that keep campaign launches consistent.
  • Always-on monitoring with alert routing to email or Slack.
  • Native connectors for Looker Studio and Power BI exports.
  • Audit-ready change tracking and version history.

Designed for modern PPC teams

Whether you manage five accounts or five hundred, Waduit scales with streamlined navigation, fast search, and multi-channel visibility. The platform surfaces context behind every recommendation so specialists and stakeholders can agree on the next move.
