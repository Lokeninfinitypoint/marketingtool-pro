Insights Desk

Always know where spend, conversions, and ROAS stand with dashboards your analysts and executives can interrogate together.

  1. Products
  2. Insights Desk

Insights Desk connects Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, and offline conversions in one command center. Build the KPIs that matter to every stakeholder, then let automations surface anomalies before they consume the budget.

Dashboards tuned to every audience

Design separate views for daily operators, channel managers, and leadership without duplicating data sources. Story-driven layouts make it simple to highlight what changed, why it matters, and what happens next.

Pacing monitors Track month-to-date spend for every campaign group and receive alerts when you're ahead or behind plan.
Performance analysis Compare segments and audiences with interactive charts that refresh as soon as new conversions arrive.
Executive summaries Package KPIs, annotations, and forecasts into export-ready slides without leaving Waduit.

Ways teams put Insights Desk to work

  • Share a real-time pacing view with finance so adjustments happen before a campaign overruns.
  • Group assets and campaigns by strategic initiative to prove the impact of every channel.
  • Send automated snapshots to stakeholders who don't need dashboard access.
