# Campaigns

### Campaigns overview

Campaigns are the top-level containers for your advertising efforts. Each campaign holds ad groups, keywords, ads, budgets, and settings. Managing campaigns effectively ensures that your budget is aligned with your objectives and that your account structure is organized.

### Managing your campaigns

* **Create new campaigns:** Set up new campaigns with goals, budget, and targeting.
* **Edit settings:** Adjust budget, location targeting, bidding strategies, and scheduling.
* **Pause or resume:** Temporarily stop campaigns that have achieved their goals or are underperforming.
* **Duplicate campaigns:** Copy an existing campaign to experiment with different settings or markets.

### How to manage campaigns

1. Navigate to **Campaigns** under the **Manage** section.
2. Use filters to find campaigns by status, budget, or name.
3. Click a campaign to view and edit its details.
4. Adjust settings like budget, bidding strategy, and targeting.
5. Save changes and monitor performance metrics in the campaign dashboard.

### Tips

* Organize campaigns by product line, region, or marketing goal for clarity.
* Regularly review budget allocation to ensure high-performing campaigns receive more funds.
* Test different bidding strategies to find the optimal approach for your objectives.
