5 Powerful Visual Marketing Tools To Enchant Your Audience and Boost ROI

Author: Guest Author
Last Updated: April 4, 2022 | Content Marketing

Loading...


Share

As we all know, there has been a technologyshiftover the last ten years as a direct response to how audiences consume information.People prefer to use small, portable mobile devices as they are often on the move and want access to the Internet from wherever they are onwhateverthey are carrying.As a result, small businessesin every industry striveto meet the demand for multichannel visual contentto remain competitive and relevant.

In fact, a studyconductedon1,500 content creatorsreported a noticeable impact of visual content onbrand awareness, website traffic, lead generation, social media engagement, and sales.

So how do you create captivating visual content that attracts, engages, and retains your audience? In this post, I’m going to equip you to do just that. I’m going to cover:

  • Exactly what visual marketing is,
  • Five stellar tools to help you create designer-worthy content, and
  • Tips to make sure your images meet your technical optimization standards.

So first, let’s cover the basics of visual marketing.

What is visual marketing?

Because the competition for consumers’attention is fierce,brands embrace thepower of visuals to grab viewers’ attentionand engage their audience.Visual marketingmeansusing graphics,videos, infographics, apps, GIFs,memes,SlideShare presentations,animations,sketches, doodles,user-generated content,anddata visualization to educate or get amessage across.

We have all heard thata“picture is worth a thousand words.”In the digital marketing landscape, pictures and other visual content forms are also worth more impressions and higher conversion rates.

Why is visual marketing important?

When you are scrolling through yoursocial media feeds, what type of content grabs your attention? Chancesare it is some type ofengaging visual content.Social mediavisualcontent evokes an emotional reaction for viewers. It alsoyields the most engagement and best return on investment.

Since90% of the information our brains process is visual, it is crucial for your business to focus on creating engaging visual content to attract your target audience.Do you need more convincing on the value of visual content marketing?

  • 91% of peopleprefer visual over written content
  • 70% of businessesinvest money in content marketing,including visual marketing.
  • According toContent Marketing Institute’s 2019 Benchmarks, Budgets, and Trends, the majority of B2C marketers are increasing how much they spend on visual content.
  • In a 2020 Digital Marketing Skills Survey,BrainStation cites 67% of respondentslisted Instagram, an entirely visual platform, as an effective social media marketing platform.

Visual marketing tools to engage your audience and boost your ROI

As we just established, using engaging visuals on your social media channels can result in huge returns. With the right tools, creating visuals is an achievable task, even for those who are not professional graphic designers or videographers.Below are five of my favorite visual marketing tools and how to use them to create imagery and tell your brand story to connect with your customers.

Alternatives to storing visualson your website

As you can see, there is no excusenot touseimpressive visuals in yoursocial media contentmarketing,given the multitude of available tools! However, it is crucial to remember the speedatwhich audiences consume your information on your website or landing pages, and thateye-catchingvisuals can hinder the user experienceby slowing downloadtimes. If images and other visuals take too long to load, your site visitors will get frustrated and leave.

However, the solution is simple. Be sure to optimizeand compress your self-hostedvisuals with free tools such as JPEG Optimizer, Compress Now, and Tiny PNG.

Although optimizing and compressing visual content on your website and landing pages will improve site speed, you may also want toconsiderusingonline storage to free up spaceon your mobile devices or websitehosting platform.

Online storage is also a protective measure should your site experience malicious attacks that compromise or corrupt your data, information, and visual content.

The future of visual marketing

There is noquestionthatmarketing trendswill continue to shift over time as technology advances andconsumer preferenceschange.What will remain the same is the usage ofvisual contentas an integral way for brands to connect with their audiences.Be sure to align your marketing campaigns with your brand. Continue to create relevant, eye-catching, and appealing visuals to attract your target market to your messages.
