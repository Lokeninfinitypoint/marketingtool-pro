# Budget

### Budget management

The Budget tool helps you plan, allocate, and adjust your advertising budgets to make the most of your spending.

#### What it does

* Track your daily and monthly spend across accounts and campaigns.
* Set budget limits to avoid overspending.
* Reallocate funds from underperforming campaigns to those delivering better results.

#### How to use it

1. Open the **Budget** tool in the Tools section.
2. View your current spend versus budget for each campaign.
3. Adjust your daily or monthly budgets using the input fields.
4. Monitor your spend regularly and tweak allocations as needed.

#### Tips

* Align budgets with your overall marketing goals and seasonal trends.
* Use automated rules (if available) to pause campaigns that hit their budget cap.
