## Quick Access Guide

### Step 1: Login to Webflow

1. **Go to:** https://webflow.com/dashboard
2. **Click "Log In"** (top right)
3. **Enter your credentials:**
   - Email: Your Webflow account email
   - Password: Your Webflow password
4. **Click "Log In"**

### Step 2: Find Your Site

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with all your sites.

**Look for:**
- Site name: "marketingtool.pro" or "Optimatia" or similar
- Site preview thumbnail

### Step 3: Open Designer

1. **Click on your site** (the card/thumbnail)
2. **Click "Design" button** (usually top right, or in the site card)
3. **Designer opens** âœ…

**You should now see:**
- Left sidebar: Navigator/Pages/Assets panels
